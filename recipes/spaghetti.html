Homemade Spaghetti with Meatsauce

This simple recipe is sure to be a hit with the whole family! It is quick, easy and most important, DELICIOUS! If you are busy like me, this recipe can be a godsend on particularly stressful days! In under 30 minutes you can have a healthy and flavorful meal for your family.

Ingredients

  • 1LB Ground Beef
  • 3 TBSP Olive Oil
  • 1 Cup Diced Red Onion
  • 3 Minced Garlic Cloves
  • 2 TBSP Tomatoe Paste
  • 28 ounces Crushed Tomatoes
  • 1/2 TSP Dried Oregano
  • 1 Cup Vegetable Stock
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Pinch of Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1 Small Bunch Basil
  • 12 oz of Spaghetti Pasta
  • 1/2 Cup Grated Parmesean

Steps

  1. Over medium-high heat,heat olive oil in large sauce pan
  2. Once hot, add ground beef to sauce pan. Breaking up beef as it cooks
  3. Once beef is lightly browned, add in your diced onion
  4. After the onions have become translucent (about five minutes) add your garlic, tomatoe paste, oregano, and red pepper flakes
  5. After about one minute or until garlic is fragrant, pour in the vegetable stock or water
  6. Add crushed tomatoes, salt and pepper bringing sauce to a simmer.
  7. Simmer for roughly 25 minutes. Stirring occassionaly
  8. As the sauce simmers, after about 10 minutes in a separate pot, add enough water and add your pasta
  9. After about 8 minutes or until pasta is al dente, you will add the basil to the sauce
  10. Make sure to save some basil and finely cut it for topping the finished dish!

Once the basil has been added it's a good idea to let the pasta sit in the sauce for at least a minute so it can absorb the sauce and flavor!
