Chicken Parmasean Sandwhich

Crunched for time and looking for a simple and delicious meal? We got you covered! This chicken parm sandwhich is a surefire hit for the whole family! In about 35 minutes you will have a hot and tasty meal on the table! It's quick and easy as you will see!

Ingredients

  • 3 Chicken breasts cut in half length-wise
  • 1/ Cup Flour
  • 4 Eggs Whisked
  • 1 and 1/4 Cup Italian-Flavored Breadcrumbs
  • 1 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Onion Powder
  • 1 and 1/2 Teaspoon Dried Basil
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 1/2 Cup Grated Parmesean
  • 2 Cups Olive Oil
  • 6 Italian Style Loaves
  • 1/4 Cup Butter
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Garlic Salt
  • 8 Ounces Shredded Mozzarella
  • 1 and 1/2 cups Marinara Sauce
  • Small Bunch of Fresh Basil

Steps

  1. In small bowl mix together: bread crumbs, your dry ingredients minus flour, and parmesean cheese.
  2. In a shallow bowl, add your eggs and in a separate bowl add your flour.
  3. Dredge chicken in eggs,flour, eggs, then bread crumb and cheese mixture. Repeat for each cutlet.
  4. Heat oil in a pan or cast iron skill on medium high heat.
  5. Once oil is hot turn down to medium and slowly add your chicken breasts one at a time.
  6. Shallow fry the chicken for rouhgly 3-5 minutes each side or until golden brown and fully cooked inside.
  7. Remove Chicken from pan and set on sheet pan.
  8. Add Shredded Mozzarella to each chicken breast.
  9. Place chicken on pan into oven on Broil for 8 minutes or until cheese is melted.
  10. Slice buns and cover inside with butter and garlic salt. Place in oven 3-5 minutes.
  11. Heat marinara sauce in microwave for about one minute.
  12. Spoon a quarter cup of marinara on each cutlet and sprinkle with fresh basil.
  13. Serve and enjoy!
